About ALP Group

Company Overview

ALP Group draws its vision and inspiration from the late Sh. Achchhe Lal Priyadarshi (ALP), a distinguished scholar, educationist, social reformer and passionate visionary. Though he is no longer with us, his ideals and principles continue to live on, guiding and transforming both the Group and society at large.

ALP Group, led by Ms. Annu Kumari (M.Sc., B.Ed.) with Mr. Guru Bandhu (MBA, LL.B.) serving as Chief Advisor and Independent Consultant, is a new-age HR, Business Consulting and Legal Consulting firm bringing over 17 years of industry expertise.
