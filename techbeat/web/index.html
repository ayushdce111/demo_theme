About Us

OUR LEGACY (ALP GROUP INDIA)

ALP Group draws its vision and inspiration from the late SH. ACHCHHE LAL PRIYADARSHI (ALP), (MA, B.Ed. & LL. B.), a distinguished scholar, educationist, social reformer, passionate visionary and the voice of thousands. Though he is no longer with us, his ideals and principles continue to live on, guiding and transforming both the Group and society at large. His legacy remains a constant source of inspiration for our endeavours. ALP Group, led by Ms. Annu Kumari (M.Sc., B.Ed.) with Mr. Guru Bandhu (MBA, LL.B.) serving as Chief Advisor and Independent Consultant, is a new-age HR, Business Consulting and Legal Consulting firm bringing over 17 years of industry expertise.

LEADERSHIP & ADVISORY

Proprietor – Ms. Annu Kumari B.Sc. (Hons.), M.Sc., B.Ed. A seasoned business leader committed to delivering high-quality staffing solutions. Chief Advisor and Independent Consultant – Sh. Guru Bandhu (MBA- HR & Marketing) with Post MBA in International HRM & LL.B., with 17 years of industry exposure in the area of Infrastructure, Construction & Engineering Industries. We are a team of highly experienced and specialized HR professionals, Recruiters, Corporate Trainers, and Business Development Experts, supported by a network of Senior Consultants, Freelancers, Academicians, and Partner Institutions. Together, we deliver customized, high-impact solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of individuals and organizations, ensuring excellence, growth, and measurable results
